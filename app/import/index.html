Instructions

1. Getting started

First, you need to have Python installed on your computer.
To check if you have Python: Top menu > Info > About.
If you see "Not installed" you have to download Python and restart the app.
Download Python
If you want to create your file yourself you can find more informations on GitHub in the README.
GitHub

2. Choose your option

You have three options to import to Authme:

Option 1: Import from Google Authenticator with QR codes
Option 2: Import from Google Authenticator with QR codes with text
Option 3: Import from stand alone QR codes

2.1 Import from Google Authenticator with QR codes

Step 1
Export the QR codes from the Google Authenticator app: Tap on the three dots on the top right of the screen > Transfer Accounts > Export Accounts
Step 2
Save the two or more QR code with a screenshot or take a picture of them from another phone. Then transfer them to your computer.
Step 3
A popup windows will open, just select the picture(s).
Step 4
You will see a popup window again. Save the file.
Step 5
Go back to the main page: Top menu > Advanced > Import
Step 6
Click on the Choose file button, select your exported file from where you saved it. And you are done!

2.2 Import from Google Authenticator with text

Step 1
Export the QR codes from the Google Authenticator app: Tap on the three dots on the top right of the screen > Transfer Accounts > Export Accounts
Step 2
Get 3rd party QR code reader from your application store. Scan the one or two QR code and then copy the text thats outputed, and then send them to your computer.
Step 3
Paste the texs(s) into the input fields.
Step 4
You will see a popup window. Save the file.
Step 5
Go back to the main page: Top menu > Advanced > Import
Step 6
Click on the Choose file button, select your exported file from where you saved it. And you are done!

2.3 Import from stand alone QR codes

Step 1
Go to the websites you want to get the QR code from.
Step 2
Save the QR codes to your computer (take a screenshot).
Step 3
A popup windows will open, just select the picture(s).
Step 4
You will see a popup window again. Save the file.
Step 5
Go back to the main page: Top menu > Advanced > Import
Step 6
Click on the Choose file button, select your exported file from where you saved it. And you are done!
