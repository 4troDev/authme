Settings

You can configure the app setting here.


Menu

Choose a configuration menu.


Launch on startup

Start the app after the operating system loaded. The app will start on the tray.


Close app to tray

On closing the app will not quit. You can open the app from the tray menu.


Disable window capture

Disables screenshots and screen capture. You can't record or screenshot the app.


Show 2FA names

The saved 2FA names will show up. Needs restart.


Reveal code

Blurs the code, but you can still copy it. Needs restart.


Reset search after copy

Reset the search bar after you copied your code. Needs restart.


Save search history

Keep your search history. Needs restart.


Clear data

Clear password, 2FA codes and all other settings. Be careful.


Open folders

You can open the folders Authme uses to function. Editing or deleting files might cause a crash.


Status

Current status of the API. Click for all the status informations.


Version

Your current Authme version. Click for release notes.
