Settings

Menu

Choose a configuration menu.


Launch on startup

Start the app after the operating system loaded. The app will start on the tray (Windows only).


Close app to tray

On closing the app will not quit. You can open the app from the tray menu (Windows only).


Show 2FA names

The saved 2FA names will show up. Needs restart.


Reveal code

Hide the code, but you can still copy it. Needs restart.


Reset search after copy

Reset the search bar after you copied your code. Needs restart.


Open folders

You can open your Authme folder (this is where you unpackaged or installed your app) and your settings folder (this is where the app stores the settings).


Clear data

Clear password, 2FA codes and all other settings.


Status

Current status of the API, click for all the status informations.


Version

Your current Authme version, click for release notes.
